About Us

About Alagappa University

Alagappa University, located in Karaikudi, Tamil Nadu, was established in 1985 by an Act of the Tamil Nadu State Government. It is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) of India. The university aims to foster research, knowledge, and innovation across various disciplines, including Science, Education, Arts, Management, and more. With a sprawling campus, modern infrastructure, and dedicated faculty, Alagappa University continues to provide quality higher education to thousands of students.

About the Faculty Directory System

The Faculty Directory System is a centralized web-based application designed for Alagappa University. It provides an organized and searchable directory of faculty members across different departments. This system benefits both students and faculty in the following ways:

  • Easy Access: Students can easily find faculty profiles with their names, designations, departments, emails, and photos.
  • Quick Updates: Faculty can update their information through a secure admin panel.
  • Efficient Communication: Encourages collaboration between students and faculty through accessible contact information.
  • Responsive Design: Works well across all devices — desktop, tablet, and mobile.

The system is built using modern web technologies like HTML, CSS, Python (Flask), and MySQL, ensuring a seamless and interactive experience for all users.
